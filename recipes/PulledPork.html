Pulled Pork Sandwhichs

Description

The best Texas Pulled Pork

Ingredients

  • One teaspoon of vegetable oil.
  • four pound pork shoulder roast.
  • Your choice of bottled BBQ sauce.
  • Yellow mustard.
  • Apple cider vinegar.
  • Sotre bought or homemade chicken broth.
  • Brown sugar.
  • Chili powder.
  • Garlic.
  • Dried thyme.
  • One chopped onion.
  • Your choice of buns.
  • Butter.

Steps

  1. Pour oil into slow cooker, then place the roast on top of the oil.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients to the slow cooker.
  3. Cover and cook until the pork shreds easily.
  4. Shred the pork and return it to the slow cooker to combine it with the juices.
  5. Serve the pulled pork on buttered buns.
