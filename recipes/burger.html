Cheeseburger

Decription

The worlds best smash cheeseburger.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds of lean ground beef.
  • 1 finely chopped onion.
  • Your choice of cheese.
  • 1 egg.
  • Dry onion souup mix.
  • Fresh garlic.
  • garlic powder.
  • Soy sauce.
  • Worcestershire sauce.
  • Dried parsley.
  • Dried basil.
  • Dried Oregano.
  • Salt and Pepper.
  • Crushed dried rosemary.

Steps

  1. Combine all the in a bowl.
  2. Shape them into four patties.
  3. Grill the patties until the desired temprature.
