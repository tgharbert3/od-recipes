Lasagna

Description

This recipe can be a little time consuming, but the the lasgna will be worth the wait.

Ingredients

  • Meat: ground beed or Italian sausage.
  • One onion
  • Two cloves of garlic
  • Two cans of crushed Tomatoes
  • Two cans of Tomatoe sauce
  • Two cans of tomato paste
  • Two tablespoons of sugar
  • Spices such as: parsley, dried basel leaves, and Italian seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese.
  • One egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover the foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before baking.
